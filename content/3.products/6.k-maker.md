## Challenges for enterprise reference data
Organisations typically manage dozens of reference datasets — product classifications, status codes, geographic hierarchies, industry standards — spread across siloed spreadsheets, duplicated across systems, and updated without governance or traceability. The result is inconsistent reporting, integration rework, and an inability to share reference data confidently across business units.  

The most common pain points K-Maker is designed to address are:  

- **No single source of truth** - fragmented lists with conflicting definitions across reports, APIs and downstream systems
- **Limited version control or provenance** - ad-hoc spreadsheet management with no audit trail, change history or approval process
- **Brittle per-system mappings** - custom integrations that break whenever a reference list is updated, creating ongoing rework
- **No stable identifiers** — concepts with no persistent identity, making reliable cross-system linking impossible
- **High barrier to enterprise solutions** - catalogues or databases products mane be appealing but may require lots of effort to implement

### Scalability beyond vocabularies

Vocabulary management is the entry point, but K-Maker is part of KurrawongAI's broader Knowledge Graph platform and is designed to grow with your organisation's data maturity.
- **Domain ontologies** — the same platform accommodates complex OWL ontologies, modelling relationships between classes, properties and individuals across your data domain as requirements grow
- **Knowledge Graph integration** — vocabulary concepts become nodes in a broader enterprise Knowledge Graph, linking reference data to business entities, processes and metadata in a coherent, machine-readable structure
- **Flexible persistence** — link to RDF graph databases, high-availability data-replication services, or version-controlled file stores, depending on the best fit for your use-case and existing infrastructure
- **SHACL-driven custom UIs** — K-Maker's interfaces leverage our KG expertise to provide ready-to-implement features that combine into a custom solution without ground-up software development overhead
